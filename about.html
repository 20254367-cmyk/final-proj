📖 Our Story

Karl Garage started as a small workshop in Irisan, Baguio City with a mission to help fellow riders get genuine parts and honest repairs. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service motorcycle hub, but our core values remain: transparency, quality, and community.

Today, we serve hundreds of satisfied customers, from daily commuters to track day enthusiasts. Every part we sell is tested, every repair is backed by our warranty, and every rider leaves with a smile.
